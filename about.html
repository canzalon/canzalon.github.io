Who am I?

Hey there. I just graduated from university this past spring semester with a bachelors in computer science, and am currently looking for work. I am kind of hoping this pathetic excuse for a website will help in my efforts for finding employment post-graduation, along with posting my projects worked on during my time at uni. To be honest, I have not been as proactive in jump-starting my career as most, mostly due to personal issues, but am now making the best effort I can.

Despite fitting the stereotype of the socially awkward engineering major quite well via my social anxiety disorder, I do my best to be a friendly, social, and open personality when the situation calls for it. My friends describe me as compassionate, a good listener, conversationalist, and even an "emotional being." Though I may be sensitive, I typically am if the situation calls for it. One of my favorite things to do is joke around and be generally comedic about anything and everything, even some of the darkest things of life. For some of us, it's how we cope. In other words, I love to laugh. :)

If you're an employer, I hope I haven't scared you away with my auto-bio; I'd rather be honest about myself, it's easier that way. Please check out some of my sample code by going to my portfolio and following the github links to the projects, or, you can just click the "github repo" link to the left. And, of course, there's also that link to my resume. Thank you for taking the time to read this and considering me. If anything, I don't mind making new friends so feel free to contact me, if you wish! :)
